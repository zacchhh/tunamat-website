# Welcome to the Tuna Athletics Blog

We're thrilled to launch our blog — a space where we'll share updates, tips, and behind-the-scenes looks at everything we're building for fitness instructors.

## What to expect

Here's what you can look forward to reading about:

- **Product updates** — New features, improvements, and what's coming next for TunaMat and our other tools.
- **Instructor tips** — Ideas and strategies for planning better classes, from mat pilates to spin and reformer.
- **Behind the scenes** — How we think about building tools that actually help instructors in their day-to-day.

## Built by instructors, for instructors

Tuna Athletics started because we saw a gap — instructors putting incredible thought into their classes but lacking the tools to match. Every feature we build comes from real conversations with real instructors.

This blog is an extension of that. We want to share what we're learning, hear what you think, and keep building together.

Stay tuned — there's a lot more coming.
