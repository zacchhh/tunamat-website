# TunaMat Launches

It's here. [TunaMat](/tunamat) is officially live on the App Store, and we couldn't be more excited to get it into your hands.

We've been building this app because we believe fitness instructors deserve better tools. Planning a killer Mat Pilates class shouldn't mean scribbling on the back of a napkin or scrolling through endless notes on your phone. TunaMat is purpose-built to help you plan, build, and teach with confidence.

## Core first, everything else follows

If you've been following along on our [Roadmap](/roadmap), you'll know we're intentional about what we ship. Rather than rushing to pack in every feature under the sun, we're laser-focused on nailing the core essentials — the things that make TunaMat genuinely useful from day one.

That means a clean flow builder, smart segment planning, real-time muscle group analysis, and a teaching mode that actually helps you in the studio. We want these to feel rock-solid before we move on, and we think you'll feel the difference.

## About the Movement library

Let's address the elephant in the room. If you're coming from the world of contemporary Mat Pilates on social media — where new creative variations pop up every day — you might open TunaMat and think the Movement library looks a little lean.

Trust us, it's coming.

We have a huge wave of contemporary and trending movements in the pipeline. The hold-up? We haven't finished designing all their icons yet. Every movement in TunaMat gets a custom visual, and we refuse to ship something that feels half-baked. Quality over quantity, always.

In the meantime, don't forget to head to **Refresh Built-in Library** inside the app every now and then. We're constantly adding new movements behind the scenes, and a quick refresh will pull the latest into your library. It's that easy.

## Got a movement you'd love to see?

This is where you come in. If there's a movement you use in your classes that isn't in TunaMat yet, we genuinely want to hear about it. Head over to the [Feedback](/feedback) tab and let us know. We read every single submission.

And here's the thing — sharing your expertise might qualify you to become a **Creator**.

## What's a Creator?

Great question. In short, Creators are instructors who contribute to the TunaMat movement library and help shape the app for the entire community. Think of it as recognition for the teachers who go above and beyond in crafting their classes.

We're not going to spill all the details just yet — that deserves its own post. Stay tuned for our next blog where we'll dive deep into what being a Creator means, how to become one, and why we think it's going to change the game.

## Get started

If you haven't already, [download TunaMat](/tunamat) and start building your first class. Got questions? Check out the [FAQ](/faq). Got ideas? Hit us up on [Feedback](/feedback).

This is just the beginning. We're building this alongside you, and we can't wait to see what you create.
